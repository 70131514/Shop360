Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Shop360°.

About this app: Shop360° is a Final Year Project (FYP) built with bare React Native (Android) and TypeScript. It is developed for The University of Lahore — Bachelor of Computer Science & Information Technology, Batch Spring 2022–2026 — for academic submission and demonstration only. No user data is shared with any third party or used outside this project. No confidentiality is breached; data (e.g. Firebase) is used only for app functionality within the scope of this academic project.

01

Information We Collect

Personal information including name, email, and profile details

Usage data and app interactions

Device information and performance logs

Camera access for AR features only — we don't store camera feeds

Optional location data for recommendations

02

How We Use Your Information

Create and manage your account

Provide AR product visualization (ViroReact / Markerless AR)

Manage wishlists, reviews, and communications

Improve app performance and user experience

Keep your account secure

We don't sell your data.

03

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using trusted services (Firebase Auth, Firestore, Storage). We apply reasonable security practices to protect your information, though no system is completely hack-proof.

04

Sharing Your Information

We only share data when required by law, needed to provide core services, or with your explicit permission. We do not share user data with third parties for marketing or commercial use.

05

Your Rights

Control app permissions from your device

Shop360° is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect their data.
